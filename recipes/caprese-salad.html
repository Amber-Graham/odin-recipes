Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad is a classic summer dish up of a mix of mozzarella, tomatoes, fresh basil, and balsamic vinegar. Your family will be sure to LOVE this dish on a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 1 ball of fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup of balsamic vinegarette
  • 2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped

Steps

  1. Cut tomates and mozzarella.
  2. Chop basil. This step is entirely up to preference for how finely chopped you would like the basil to be!
  3. mix all ingredients together and enjoy!
